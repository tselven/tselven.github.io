How to Add Images to Your Blog Posts

To add images to your blog posts, you can:

  1. Include images in the frontmatter - Add an image field to the frontmatter that will be used as the Open Graph image for social sharing.

Benefits of Adding Images

  • Improves visual appeal
  • Enhances social media sharing
  • Makes content more engaging
  • Helps break up text

Images in the frontmatter will be used for Open Graph tags when someone shares your post on social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, or LinkedIn.
